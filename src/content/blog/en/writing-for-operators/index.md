The best technical writing shortens the distance between confusion and the next correct action.

## Lead with the question

People usually open documentation because something is unclear, slow, or failing.

### Keep commands close to context

Readers should not need to scroll through theory to find the next useful step.

## Prefer repair over ceremony

The right note at the right moment is worth more than a beautifully structured document nobody uses.
