Operational complexity often hides inside the path a request takes through a system.

## Keep each hop explainable

Every extra decision point adds a little more uncertainty for the team.

### Prefer a visible default path

When the normal route is obvious, exceptions become easier to notice and reason about.

## Make debugging local first

The best routing design helps someone answer a failure quickly from logs, traces, and simple checks.
