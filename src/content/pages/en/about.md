This site is mostly about service boundaries, infrastructure routing, and systems that remain explainable while they are being operated.

I care less about impressive diagrams and more about structures that teams can keep for a long time.

## What I write about

- Backend architecture
- TSS MPC development
- Infrastructure routing and operations

## Why I write

Because the best technical writing does not make systems look smarter. It helps people understand them faster and repair them with more confidence.
